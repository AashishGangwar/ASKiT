1. How does ASKiT streamline Q&A sessions during events?

ASKiT uses advanced AI and QR code integration to collect and manage audience questions in real-time. Attendees scan a QR code to submit questions, which are prioritized based on relevance, ensuring a seamless and engaging Q&A experience.

2. What types of events are best suited for ASKiT?

ASKiT is designed for a variety of events, including corporate seminars, academic workshops, conferences, and panel discussions. Its customizable features make it adaptable to different audience sizes and event formats.

3. How does the post-event chatbot work?

Our AI-powered chatbot remains active for up to eight hours after the event, addressing any follow-up questions or doubts participants may have. It ensures continuous engagement and helps provide a complete learning experience.

4. What kind of insights does the Audience Analytics tool provide?

The Audience Analytics tool provides data on question trends, participant engagement levels, and common topics of interest. It helps organizers identify gaps, refine their content, and improve future event strategies.

5. Is ASKiT easy to integrate with existing event setups?

Absolutely! ASKiT offers user-friendly tools and seamless QR code integration, making it easy to incorporate into your event. Our platform is compatible with live, hybrid, and virtual event formats, ensuring a hassle-free experience.
